Activities & Events

Beyond The Classroom

School is more than lessons. These are the moments children carry with them long after they leave.

More Than Academics

Learning through discovery

A child's school years are defined by far more than what happens in the classroom. At Debsteph Hall, we invest deeply in a school calendar full of experiences — events that build confidence, celebrate identity, reward hard work, and bring the whole school community together.

From the energy of Sports Day to the pride of Graduation, every event here is deliberate and well-executed. These aren't just nice additions to the school year. They are part of what makes a Debsteph Hall education memorable.

Second
Term
Annual Event
Cultural Day

Cultural Day is one of the most vibrant days in our school calendar. The school fills with colour, music, and pride as students come dressed in traditional attire from tribes across Nigeria. It's a celebration of who we are — our diversity, our heritage, and the beauty of belonging to a community that honours all of it. Teachers join in, families come out, and the atmosphere is genuinely electric.

What happens on the day
Students dress in traditional attire from their tribe or family heritage
Cultural performances — music, dance, storytelling, and poetry
Teachers and staff join the celebration fully
Family attendance and dedicated photography time
End of Year
Graduation Ceremony

Graduation at Debsteph Hall is a proper ceremony — formal, dignified, and deeply meaningful. When a child walks across that stage in their gown and cap, it represents years of effort, growth, and the belief we have placed in them every step of the way. For families, it's a milestone. For the school, it's a moment of genuine pride. We don't take it lightly.

What happens on the day
Formal procession in graduation gowns and caps
Certificate and award presentation before the full school community
Speeches from students and school leadership
Photography and family celebration time after the ceremony

Inter-House Sports Day

There is nothing quite like Sports Day. The houses come alive — kits, chants, and a genuine competitive spirit that brings out something special in every student. From the youngest sprinter to the relay anchor, every child has a role to play and a moment to shine. It's athletic, it's loud, and it's one of the days students talk about for weeks afterwards.

What happens on the day
Track events — 100m, 200m and relay races across all age groups
Field events — long jump, high jump, and more
House colour kits and cheering squads for every team
Trophy and medal presentation ceremony to close the day
Annual
End-of-Year Concert

The most anticipated night of the school year. Every class performs — songs, dances, drama skits — and every child gets their moment on stage. Families fill the hall, costumes are on point, and the energy is unlike anything else we do. It's the perfect way to close the year: loud, joyful, and impossible not to smile through.

What happens on the night
Songs, dances, and drama skits from every class in the school
Solo and group musical acts from across the year groups
Costume displays and themed presentations
Refreshments and family celebration to close the evening

Open Day

Open Day is for every family considering Debsteph Hall — and for those already with us. Classrooms are open, teachers are available, and the school is at its best. Prospective parents get to see exactly what we mean when we talk about our environment, our staff, and how we work. No brochure captures it better than walking through and seeing it for yourself.

What happens on the day
Guided tours of classrooms and all school facilities
Meet-and-greet with teachers and school leadership
Live classroom demonstrations showing learning in action
Admissions information and enrolment support available on the day
Ceremony
Prize Giving Day

Prize Giving Day is how we say to every child who worked hard this year: we saw you. Academic excellence, consistent effort, strong character — all of it is formally recognised before the whole school community. It matters to children. It matters to families. And it sends a clear message about what this school values: not just results, but the person behind them.

What happens on the day
Academic achievement awards presented per class
Character and conduct commendations recognised publicly
Subject-specific prizes across all academic areas
Certificates presented by school leadership

Every Week

Clubs & Regular Activities

Beyond the major events, these sessions are woven into every student's school week throughout the year.

Music & Singing

Weekly sessions where children learn songs, basic rhythm, and vocal expression in a fun group setting.

Creative Arts

Painting, drawing, craft and sculpture — children explore their creativity with hands-on projects every week.

Debate & Public Speaking

Structured discussion sessions that build confidence, critical thinking, and the ability to communicate clearly.

Sports & Athletics

Regular physical education including football, relay, and team sports that develop fitness and sportsmanship.

Reading Club

Guided reading that fosters a genuine love of books, expands vocabulary, and develops comprehension from early on.

Computer & Technology

Practical sessions introducing digital literacy, basic typing, and age-appropriate technology skills.

Drama & Theatre

Acting, improvisation, and stagecraft that bring out children's creative and performative confidence.

Values & Character

Weekly character education circles exploring integrity, empathy, and what it means to be a responsible young person.
