Our Programmes

What Do We Offer?

A complete educational pathway, from a child's very first school day through to the end of primary.

Built for Every Stage

Structured for nurturing
little minds

Every programme at Debsteph Hall is built with one goal in mind — giving your child the strongest possible foundation for the years ahead. We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. Each stage of learning here is carefully structured to meet children where they are, and move them forward with confidence.

From the first day a toddler walks through our doors to the morning a Primary 6 pupil finishes their final term, the experience is thoughtful, personal, and genuinely invested in the child.

Pre-school
Where it all begins

The earliest years of a child's life are the most formative — and we treat them that way. Pre-school at Debsteph Hall is a warm, play-rich environment where children develop at their own pace, with patient teachers who genuinely know each child by name and nature. We're not rushing them. We're building the right foundation.

What your child gets
Play-based learning that builds early cognitive and social skills naturally
Sensory and creative activities — art, music, and structured exploration every day
Small class sizes so every child is known, seen, and supported
Consistent daily routines that give children emotional security and confidence

Nursery
The academic journey begins

Nursery is where play gives way to structure — and children begin discovering that learning can be both serious and genuinely enjoyable. We introduce phonics, early reading, and numeracy through hands-on activities designed to spark curiosity rather than create pressure. By the time a child leaves our nursery, they are ready — and eager — for primary school.

What your child gets
Phonics and early reading for a strong, lasting literacy foundation
Practical numeracy through activities that make numbers make sense
Creative arts and group projects to build collaboration and expression
Daily story time and oral comprehension to spark imagination

Primary School
Where potential becomes achievement

Our primary programme is rigorous, comprehensive, and rooted in character. We hold high standards across all core subjects while making sure every child actually understands — not just keeps up. Pupils leave Debsteph Hall Primary with academic confidence, strong study habits, and the values to carry them well beyond the classroom.

What your child gets
Core subjects taught in depth — English, Mathematics, Basic Science, and Social Studies
Computer Studies and digital literacy introduced at an early stage
Termly assessments with detailed report cards and structured parent feedback
Character education — integrity, civic responsibility, and leadership values built in

Summer School
Keeping momentum during the break

We also run a Summer School programme during the long holiday break. It's our way of making sure children don't lose the progress they've worked hard for — and that they actually enjoy the time. Themed activity weeks, creative projects, outdoor sessions, and optional revision come together in a structured programme that keeps young minds active and ready for the term ahead. It's the full Debsteph Hall experience, just in the holidays.

What your child gets
Themed activity weeks — Science, Arts, Nature, and more each holiday
Creative projects and outdoor activities for every age group
Optional academic revision for children preparing for the new term
Sports and games to keep children active and energised
