A Foundation
for Excellence

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Debsteph Hall School. As proprietress, I am committed to providing young learners with a strong educational foundation in a safe and supportive environment.

We combine quality teaching with personal attention, ensuring each child develops strong academic skills alongside confidence, curiosity, and good character.

From preschool through primary, we focus on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking, while nurturing the social and emotional growth that helps children truly thrive.

Mrs. Oluwakemi Babalola
Proprietress, Debsteph Hall School
