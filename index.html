A Foundation for Excellence

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Debsteph Hall School. As proprietress, I am committed to providing young learners with a strong educational foundation in a safe and supportive environment.

We understand that the early years of education are critical in shaping your child's future. At Debsteph Hall, we combine quality teaching with personalized attention, ensuring each child develops strong academic skills alongside confidence, curiosity, and good character. Our dedicated teachers create engaging lessons that make learning enjoyable while instilling discipline and a genuine love for knowledge.

From our preschool to primary classes, we focus on building essential skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking, while nurturing the social and emotional development that helps children thrive. We maintain a structured yet caring atmosphere where every child is encouraged to participate, ask questions, and discover their abilities.

I invite you to visit our school in Bogije and see how we can partner with you in your child's educational journey.

Mrs. Oluwakemi Babalola
Proprietress, Debsteph Hall School
