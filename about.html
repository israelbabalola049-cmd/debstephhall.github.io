Our Story

Debsteph Hall School was founded in 2019 with a single, clear purpose: to create a school where every child is truly seen. Situated in Bogije, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos, the school was born out of a deep conviction that quality early education should be warm, intentional, and rooted in genuine care for the child as a whole person.

From our first intake of pupils, we established a culture of excellence balanced with compassion. Our proprietress brought together a team of dedicated educators committed to combining strong academic teaching with personal attention and character formation.

Today, Debsteph Hall serves families from across Bogije, Lekki, Ajah, and the wider Ibeju-Lekki corridor, offering programmes from Pre-school through Primary.

What We Stand For

Vision & Mission

Our Vision
Raising generations ready for the world ahead.

To be the most trusted and nurturing school community in Ibeju-Lekki — raising generations of confident, compassionate, and academically strong young Nigerians who are prepared for the world ahead.

Our Mission
Education that is rigorous, personal, and joyful.

To provide every child with an exceptional early education — one that combines rigorous academics with personal care, character development, and a safe, joyful environment where each child can grow to their full potential.
